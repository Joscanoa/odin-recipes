Tallarin Verde

Tallarin verde is a special dish that is unique and tasty just like the Causa, although we do use milk so we will be putting this warning for those who are lactose intolerent. Anyways this Dish is from peru and it has the same warm feeling one could get during a cold winter night. It is tasty and also a bit messy, anyways lets get started with this Dish, and quite simple.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of spinach
  • 1 cup of basil
  • 5 ounces of queso fresco
  • 2 garlic cloves peeled
  • 1/4 shallot
  • 1/2 walnut
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/4 milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pepper
  • pasta

Steps

  1. blend the spinach, basil, cheese, garlic, walnut, oil, milk, salt for 30 minutes adding salt and pepper when needed and youre done! that is the sauce, of course you know how to make pasta im assuming, just add the sauce ont the pasta
