Causa

The Dish, we are going over is a Peruvian cuisine, that features a variety of flavors that come together to establish this dish. A fresh taste that is both delicous and unique, if you love tuna, potato, and some good avocados than this is the dish for you. It is for sure one of those dishes that you must try. As we continue on we will be advicing you to properly add the ingredients specified, and strongly suggest that you get the exact condiments, specially when it comes to mayonaise, that is all hope you enjoy both the process and the result of your cooking.

Ingredients

  • can of tuna
  • 3 pounds of potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • a half onion cut into slices
  • 3 yellow chili peppers
  • tablespoon of mayo, (recommend mayo with hint of lime)
  • 2 lime
  • an avocado
  • 2 hard boiled eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Parsley
  • 1 limo chili pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the potatoes put the potatoes in a pot with water adding Salt boil and cook until tender which should be around 20 minutes
  2. Mash the potatoes let it cool and then smash as you are smashing add lime and oil spread it baking the layers
  3. Tuna in a bowl we can spread the tuna that has been drained and mix with our chopped onion, lime, salt, and pepper
  4. Assembleline up the tuna over a layer of potato, adding the the layer of potato on top of the tuna mixed substancead adjusting the filling and the potato layer finish up
its ready to serve Home
