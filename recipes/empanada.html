Empanada

Empanadas a delicacy all over, warm and tasty, a good empanada can have feeling the warmth of a nice old lady who has been preparing soup on a cold winter night. A good empanada is such a treat to have as it is very little it could be considered a snack or have multiple and call it lunch. Very popilaur with latinos and hispanic as it is a dish, many different countries like to put their own spin to it.

Ingredients

  • 4 flour cups
  • 1 1/2 teaspot fine Salt
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup of vegetable
  • 2 table spoons of butter
  • 2 large egg yolk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 pound of chicken breast
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 oil
  • 2 large onion
  • 1 spoon paprika
  • 1 spoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 spoon chilipowder
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 3 eggs cooked
  • 1/2 cup sliced green olives
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 spoon water

Steps

  1. Make the Dough place the flour in a medium bowl and stir salt and sugar add vegetable to have a sandy mixture in another bowl mix eggs and water 3/4 after we will mix it with our dough knead it all together now it is ready to refrigiraye and move on
  2. Chicken preperation place the chicken in medium saucepan crumble the bay leaf on top cover with water and boil. Once it is boiling we cook it for 20 minutes the 20 minutes. Once those are up we shred the chicken into small pieces
  3. Filling Heat up in a skillet onion paprika cumin chili powder amd salt and pepper add some water and cook for 15 minutes remove the mixture and let it cool and it is ready
  4. Now we are ready to roll out the doughs with the filling, and vuala empanadas!
